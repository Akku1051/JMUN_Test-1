Conference Itinerary.

The JMUN 2026 conference day follows one clear itinerary from arrival and registration to closing ceremonies.

8:00-8:20

Arrival and registration

Delegates check in and receive conference materials.

8:30-9:00

Opening ceremonies

The conference begins with welcome remarks and delegate orientation.

9:00-10:20

Committee Session 1

Delegates begin formal debate and caucus work.

10:20-10:30

Break

A short pause between committee sessions.

10:30-11:40

Committee Session 2

Committees continue debate, collaboration, and drafting.

11:40-12:20

Lunch

Delegates break before the final committee session.

12:20-1:35

Committee Session 3

Final debate, voting, and committee wrap-up.

1:40-2:20

Closing ceremonies

Awards and final remarks close JMUN 2026.

JMUN 2026 at St. Joseph Secondary School, Mississauga.
