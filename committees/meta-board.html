Meta Board of Directors

Delegates act as directors, executives, shareholders, and regulators shaping the strategy of the company behind Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, and more.

Committee Focus

This committee asks delegates to balance rapid innovation, financial growth, and ethical responsibility in a dynamic digital age.

Topics

  • Data collection, user surveillance, and associated financial costs
  • Content moderation vs. free speech
  • Mental health protection on social media
