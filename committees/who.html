Committees/WHO

Specialized Committee

World Health Organization

WHO connects nations, partners, and individuals to promote health and coordinate global responses to health emergencies.

Committee Focus

Delegates balance the wellbeing of people around the world with healthcare demands while developing strategies to prevent the spread of viral and bacterial diseases.

Topics

  • Determining and mitigating the root causes of viral outbreaks and pandemics worldwide
  • Enhancing vector control strategies in extreme and remote environments

Details

  • Chair: Tvisha Rathee, Under-Secretary-General of Crisis
  • Seconder: Namya Pandey
