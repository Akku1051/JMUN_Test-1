About the conference

Built for thoughtful debate and confident first steps.

JMUN 2026 brings students together to explore global issues through research, diplomacy, and collaboration. Delegates will represent countries, form blocs, draft resolutions, and practice the kind of public speaking that stays useful long after committee closes.

The conference is designed to feel welcoming for newer delegates while still giving experienced students meaningful room to challenge themselves in committee.
